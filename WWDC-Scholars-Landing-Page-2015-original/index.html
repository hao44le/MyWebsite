What is it?

An app to showcase the winners of the WWDC 15 Scholarship.

Detail

Has detailed information on every scholar as well as a short biography.

Map

Has a page for displaying all the locations of the scholars around the world.

Screenshots

Has pictures of the app that each scholar has made to win the scholarship.

Across all Devices

You can view information on any device.

Edit

Ability to edit information. This feature is available to scholars only.

Contact Information

Has contact information of every scholar.
