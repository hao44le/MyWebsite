About US

Skylight Investment focuses on investing in early-stage companies with disruptive technologies. We actively seek entrepreneurs with a track record of success. Our primary focus includes robotics, biotechnology, hardware, artificial intelligence, blockchain, novel materials, and other advanced technologies. We invest in seed round and series A round, our typical investment is between $100,000 and $500,000.

Skylight Investment is supported by well-established Chinese partners including Taiyou Fund and  New Oriental (NYSE:EDU). We strive to assist our portfolio companies in reaching a global market.

    IPO with Market Capitalization of $1.9 Billion: China Shengmu Organic Milk Limited

    Shengmu Organic Milk is a leader in the Chinese organic milk industry. In 2009, Mr. Fangzhen Sun, founder of China Shengmu Organic Milk Limited, achieved embryonic hybridization of Australia cattle and Chinese cattle.
